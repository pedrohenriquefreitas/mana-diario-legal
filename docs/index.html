🎵 TikTok Integration

Mana Diario connects to TikTok through the official TikTok for Developers platform to publish devotional video content to authorized creator accounts. The integration uses the following products and scopes:

  • Login Kit Authenticates the creator account owner via TikTok OAuth 2.0. The account owner clicks "Connect TikTok", is redirected to TikTok's authorization page, approves the requested permissions, and is redirected back to the platform. No content is published or accessed until the owner explicitly authorizes the app.
  • Content Posting API Used to upload and publish short devotional video reels to the authorized TikTok profile. Before each publish, the platform presents a confirmation screen where the creator can review content, set privacy level, and configure interaction settings (comments, duet, stitch) and commercial content disclosures.
  • Share Kit Allows sharing pre-built video content packages from the platform to TikTok on behalf of the authorized creator. Used as an alternative publish path when the creator prefers to initiate the share action manually from the dashboard.
  • user.info.basic Reads the connected account's public profile information (open ID, display name, avatar URL) to confirm which TikTok account is linked and display it in the platform dashboard. No private data is accessed.
  • video.publish Directly posts finalized devotional video reels to the creator's TikTok profile. Used only after the creator has reviewed and approved the content. The platform never publishes without explicit account owner authorization.
  • video.upload Uploads video content to TikTok as a draft, allowing the creator to review the content inside TikTok's own editor and make final edits or adjustments before deciding to publish to their audience.
