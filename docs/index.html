Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mana Diario / OpenClaw Social ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when managing connected creator and brand accounts.

  • Mana Diario (@manadiariohoje) - A Brazilian Portuguese devotional content channel
  • Fierce by Alinne (@alinnecfreitas) - A women's fitness and empowerment brand

2. Information We Collect

Through integration with third-party platform APIs, the Service may access:

  • Public profile information - Username, display name, avatar, bio information, and other profile fields exposed by authorized platforms
  • Comments and messages - Content of comments on our posts and direct messages sent to our managed accounts
  • Page insights - Aggregated analytics about post performance, reach, and engagement
  • Media content - Images and text content published by our managed accounts
  • Connected account authorization data - Access tokens, refresh tokens, account IDs, and configuration data required to operate approved integrations

3. How We Use Information

Information accessed through Meta's APIs is used solely for:

  • Responding to comments and direct messages on our managed social media accounts
  • Creating and publishing content (posts, carousels, stories) to our managed accounts
  • Analyzing post performance to improve content strategy
  • Managing content scheduling and publishing workflows
  • Preparing creator-ready drafts, scripts, and teleprompter-friendly assets where supported

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on private, access-controlled infrastructure
  • API credentials and access tokens are stored in encrypted environment variables, never in public repositories
  • No personal data is sold or rented to third parties
  • Comment and message data is retained only as long as necessary for engagement management

5. AI-Assisted Interactions

The Service uses AI models to assist with:

  • Generating caption text for social media posts
  • Drafting replies to comments and messages
  • Analyzing content trends and performance
  • Generating scripts, hooks, and creator guidance for optional human-recorded content

AI-generated responses follow strict brand guidelines and forbidden topic filters. No personal user data is used to train AI models.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • Meta Platform (Facebook/Instagram) - For publishing content and managing engagement via the Graph API
  • TikTok for Developers - For account connection, creator data access, and content posting workflows where enabled
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) - For AI-assisted content generation and reply drafting

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

7. Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their data by contacting us. To request removal of any comment or message data we have stored, or to exercise your rights under applicable data protection laws, please reach out using the contact information below.

8. Platform Data Deletion

In compliance with platform requirements, you may request deletion of data obtained through connected services such as Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok by contacting us. We will delete stored data related to your connected account within a reasonable period after receiving a verified request, subject to legal or security retention requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our managed accounts create content for general adult audiences.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
